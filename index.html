Growth Metric: CAGR Formula

We use the Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR) to evaluate long-term performance:

$$ CAGR = \left( \frac{V_{\text{final}}}{V_{\text{initial}}} \right)^{\frac{1}{t}} - 1 $$

Where:

  • $V_{\text{final}}$: Ending Value
  • $V_{\text{initial}}$: Beginning Value
  • $t$: Number of time periods (years)
To put our long-term growth into perspective, we consistently track our CAGR. This formula helps us smooth out market volatility and understand the true, compounded growth rate over time. Our 5-year revenue CAGR stands at an impressive 14.5%.
